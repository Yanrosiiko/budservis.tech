The "Turn-Key" reconstruction and renovation of the TPS "Al-Hiswa" in the Aden, Republic of Yemen
Our current project has the great perspectives for future development because it great value for Yemen energy system. We strive to widespread the energy possibilities for Yemen economy.
Some FACTS about TPS "Al-Hiswa":
The "Al-Hiswa" Thermal Power Station (TPS "Al-Hiswa") which is located in the suburb of Aden "Madina Al-Shaab" was built by Soviet specialists in 1991 as part of the all-Union assistance for the People's Democratic Republic of Yemen (South Yemen).
The main task was to match necessary accessories from modern producers with which it would be possible to replace the Soviet analogs which failed and fulfilled the resource.
The TPS "Al-Hiswa" has a nominal capacity of 125 MW (5 blocks of 25MW). It includes 5 operating units.
The TPS "Al-Hiswa" is a complete analogue of many such objects, which were built in the USSR.
Boilers, steam generators and turbines at the TPS "Al-Hiswa" represent a GOST development, but have characteristic features, which also complicates the reconstruction work.
The complexity of the project on reconstruction of Thermal Power Station "Al-Hiswa" is to maximize the preservation of existing elements, structural materials, spare parts and details.
During the reconstruction of TPS "Al-Hiswa" it is important to comply with the Soviet GOST standards, and to modernize the equipment in accordance with modern EU and US standards.
Of course, the Company, conducting a similar business in the Republic of Bangladesh and the Islamic Republic of Afghanistan had high experience of such works.
In the framework of the General Contract for "turn-key" reconstruction it has to be executed the next changes/rebuilds and totally replacements:
Turbines (turbine blades, electrical generating circuits, electric loops, systems of sound and vibration isolation).
All control and measuring instruments of the TPS (thermometers, flame sensors, vibration diagnostics, electronic combustor control systems, speed indicators, control and start-up systems, fire, emergency stop systems, etc.).
Almost all the system pipe lines (wiring of the gas burners of the boiler combustion, economizers, heat and insulation, oil system, with oil boiler, a system of introduction of refrigerant from the boiler, and almost all of the pipeline system supplying power boiler, etc.).
Anti-corrosion treatment and renovation of all technological premises of the TPS from exhaust gas pipes to almost all premises: boiler room, turbine hall, control and control room, etc.
Partial repair work on the site is required:
Repair of roof and wall holes of the power unit building.
Pouring concrete screed and leveling the floor in the warehouse premises for fuel oil power station.
Most of the replacement of power and electrical networks. Since service life in a hot and humid climate long ago expired.
Most of the spare parts of transformers, high voltage generators, electric current distributors.
According to the terms of the Contract, the Company performs all this work for 2,5 years.
