Building, reconstruction and renovation in Hydroenergy
The LLC "BIC "Budservis" has the great experience in reconstruction and renovation of the HPS in Ukraine and IR Afganistan. We are ready for the next cooperation in complex projects as well as for realization of every complicated decision in Hydroenergy.
The "Budservis", Construction and Investment Co. LLC is the associated partner of the all-Ukrainian State association OJSC “Ukrhydroproject. In past cooperation it was executed cooperation renovation and rehabilitation projects.
The profile of activity of joint activity is extensive and includes service, reconstruction and repair:
  • cascades of hydroelectric power stations on the rivers Dnieper and Dniester;
  • hydroelectric power stations abroad;
  • constructions of reservoirs;
  • large channels in Donbass and Dnieper;
  • navigable constructions;
  • hydrotechnical constructions of objects of the mining industry, power lines, substations, bridges, roads, the enterprises of building industry;
At all stages of development, designing complex and unique structures, the company initiated the implementation of many technical solutions developed and implemented for the first time in the country, which provided not only significant cost savings, but also new quality.
Some examples of our last experience works in Hydroenergy branch:
Ukraine. The finish start up works of the main switch engine, the reconstruction of the flood systems of the Dniprovsaya HydroPowerStation;
Ukraine. Removal, capital repair, and installing works of the main generator rotor in the Kakhovskaya HydroPowerStation;
Egypt. Reconstruction of Aswan HydroPowerStation generating sets: total substitution of all control-measurement equipment, works for automatization adjustment;
Ukraine. Welding works at the hydraulic unit runner, full included works for substitance and assembly of the hydraulic machine equipment units, supplies and adjustment works of the regulating ring of the hydraulic unit, etc.
Vietnam. Project and design works for reconstruction as well as the design and planning works for «turn-key» construction of the «Thak Mo» HPS (150MW), «Can Don» HPS (77.6MW), «Yali» HPS (720MW), «Sesan» (300MW) HPS, Nam Chien HPS (200MW).
